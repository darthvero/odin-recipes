Sopa De Fideo

A Childhood Comfort Food

Fideo soup is what Mexican childhood memories are made of. It is a warm comforting soup made with a tangy tomato base and golden brown noodles. It is so popular with moms because it is very easy to make, and kids love it because what kid doesn't like noodles?

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fideo pasta (or vermicelli, angel hair pasta)
  • 2 small tomatoes (or ½ cup of canned tomato sauce)
  • ½ medium onion
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 4 ¼ cups chicken stock (or water + 3 tsp of chicken buoillon)
  • 3 Tbsp canola oil
  • Salt (by preference)

Instructions

  1. Cut tomatoes in half and place them with the onion and garlic in a blender.
  2. Add 1 cup of chicken stock and blend until pureed.
  3. Preheat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Add pasta and stir to coat with oil. Cook stirring constantly until the pasta is nicely browned.
  5. Pour the tomato sauce. Let it sizzle while mixing for about half a minute.
  6. Add remaining chicken stock. Season with salt and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes or until fideo pasta is well cooked.
